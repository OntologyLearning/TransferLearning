Demos & Posters Session

To complement the main conference program, HT2011 will hold a combined Demonstrations and Posters session. The purpose of this session is to provide the authors with the ability to demonstrate their developments, to tell more about their research and results to interested participants; to involve participants in discussions about their work, exchanging ideas, providing feedback.

The submissions for Demos & Posters session must be related to the topics of interest of HT2011 main research tracks. Authors submitting a paper to HT2011 conference track may consider submitting the same work to Demos & Posters session, for example if they want to provide a demo or a poster for an accepted paper. Note, that a separate demo or poster submission must be made. A first draft of what the poster will look like should also be submitted.

  • Posters can be used to present completed work but also work in progress that is not yet sufficiently mature for the research track.
  • Through demonstrations new tools and innovative applications of hypertext can be presented.

All submissions should be formatted according to the official ACM SIG proceedings template and submitted via EasyChair. Submissions should include an extended abstract of maximum 2 pages, including all figures and references. It should state the contribution and originality of the work explicitly, including just enough background to make it clear how the work differs from significant prior work. A full literature review is not required.

Unfortunately the submissions cannot be included in the proceedings.
