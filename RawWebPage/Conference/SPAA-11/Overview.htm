23rd ACM Symposium on Parallelism in Algorithms and Architectures

San Jose, California, USA
June 4 - 6, 2011

Registration to SPAA is now open.

The SPAA program is now available.

The 23rd Annual ACM Symposium on Parallelism in Algorithms and Architectures (SPAA '11) is sponsored by the ACM Special Interest Groups on Algorithms and Computation Theory (SIGACT) and Computer Architecture (SIGARCH) and organized in cooperation with the European Association for Theoretical Computer Science. SPAA '11 is part of the 2011 Federated Computing Research Conference (FCRC 2011), June 4-11, 2011. SPAA 2011 will feature regular papers, each with a 25-minute talk, and brief announcements, each with a 10-minute talk. The SPAA brief announcements are for brief communications including work in progress or demos.
