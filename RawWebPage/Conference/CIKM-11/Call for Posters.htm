Call for Posters

Poster presentations provide researchers with a unique opportunity to present and to receive direct feedback from an expert audience about significant work in progress, bleeding-edge work, or research that is best communicated in an interactive or graphical fashion.

We invite you to submit an extended abstract of no longer than 4 pages for poster presentation, in the general areas of databases, information retrieval, and knowledge management, especially posters bridging these areas, or presenting new perspectives in these areas. A full list of current topics of interest can be found at http://www.cikm2011.org/callforpapers.

All extended abstracts for posters will be reviewed and published in the ACM CIKM proceedings, which will be distributed in CD-ROM at the conference. Submissions of extended abstracts for posters must be in English and should not exceed 4 pages in ACM CIKM Proceedings format including references and figures. The ACM camera-ready templates are available at: http://www.acm.org/sigs/publications/proceedings-templates.

As with full papers, authors are required to anonymize their submission and conceal their identity. Poster papers must be submitted electronically via the posters submission web page.

Important Dates for Poster Submissions
Poster papers due: May 30, 2011
Notification: July 26, 2011
Camera ready: August 12, 2011

Late submissions will be rejected without further consideration.

Queries regarding poster submissions should be sent to the Posters Chair: Gianni Amati (posters@cikm2011.org)
