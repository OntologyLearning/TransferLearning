36th International Symposium on Mathematical Foundations of Computer Science

The series of MFCS symposia, organized in rotation by Poland, Slovakia, and the Czech Republic since 1972, has a long and well-established tradition. The MFCS symposia encourage high-quality research in all branches of theoretical computer science. Their broad scope provides an opportunity to bring together researchers who do not usually meet at specialized conferences.

As in the last year, Young Researchers Forum will be organized in August 22-24 as an event associated with MFCS: http://mfcs.mimuw.edu.pl/yrf
