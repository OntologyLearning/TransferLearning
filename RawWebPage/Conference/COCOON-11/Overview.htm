Welcome to the 17th Annual International Computing and Combinatorics Conference!

The 17th Annual International Computing and Combinatorics Conference (COCOON'11) will be held in the city of Dallas, Texas, USA, during August 14-16, 2011.

The conference organizers would like to extend their most cordial invitation to researchers around the world to come to one of the most beautiful city in north Texas, to exchange their most significant research works.
