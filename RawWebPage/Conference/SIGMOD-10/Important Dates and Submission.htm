Camera-ready submission instructions

Complete instructions for preparing the final version of SIGMOD and PODS accepted papers are available here.

 

Draft Submission Instructions

All aspects of the submission and notification process will be handled electronically. Submissions to the SIGMOD Research, Demo, and Industry tracks are made through the submission site (submission to all other tracks is by E-Mail). The SIGMOD Submission site is:

https://cmt.research.microsoft.com/SIGMOD2010

Submissions to the PODS Research track are made through the PODS Submission site:

http://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=pods2010

Please read all of the requirements carefully well in advance of preparing your submission. Please note that you can and should register your paper with the submission site well before the final deadline, even if the paper itself is not ready. You may upload the paper later. When a paper is registered through the submission site, relevant information will be collected, including contact author information. Please choose contact author information (particularly email address) that will be stable through June 2010.
