PAPER SUBMISSION

Submissions should include abstract, 5-10 keywords, and the e-mail address of the corresponding author and be in PDF format. Each submission must not exceed 8 pages in the IEEE 8.5'' x 11'' two-column format with 10-12 point font, including tables and figures.

Each submission should be regarded as an undertaking that, should the submission be accepted, at least one of the authors must register the paper and attend the conference to present the work in order that the accepted paper can be put into digital library. The final version will be limited to 8 pages in IEEE proceeding format for conference papers and 6 pages for workshop papers.

Full paper should be submitted to electronic submission system before June 24, 2011.
