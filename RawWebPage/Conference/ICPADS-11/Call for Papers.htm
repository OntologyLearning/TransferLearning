CALL FOR PAPERS

Established in 1992, ICPADS has been a major international forum in the parallel and distributed systems area. ICPADS 2011 will be held in Tainan, Taiwan in December 2011.Tainan is a wonderful historic city in Taiwan that has many places to visit. The conference provides an international forum for scientists, engineers and users to exchange and share their experiences, new ideas, and latest research results on all aspects of parallel and distributed systems.

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Parallel and Distributed Applications and Algorithms
  • Cloud OS, Middleware, Toolkits, and Applications
  • Data Intensive Computing and Data Centre Architecture
  • Web-Based Computing and Service-Oriented Architecture
  • Multi-core and Multithreaded Architectures
  • Virtualization Techniques
  • Resource Provision, Monitoring, and Scheduling
  • Security and Privacy
  • Cluster and Grid Computing
  • Power-Aware and Green Computing
  • Internet of Things
  • Peer-to-Peer Computing
  • Wireless and Mobile Computing
  • Ad Hoc and Sensor Networks
  • Performance Modeling and Evaluation
  • Communication and Networking Systems
  • Dependable and Trustworthy Computing and Systems
  • Real-Time and Multimedia Systems
  • High Performance Computational Biology and Bioinformatics
  • Cyber-Physical Systems

ICPADS 2011 will accommodate full-day workshops. These workshops will enable timely important issues to be explored in depth. A workshop proposal should include organizer's contact information, the workshop title, rationale, coverage, how workshop papers will be evaluated, and experience of past workshops on the same or closely related subjects.

IMPORTANT DATES :

  • Deadline for workshop proposals : June 10, 2011
  • Notification for workshop organizers : June 20, 2011
  • Deadline for paper submissions : June 24, 2011
  • Notification of paper acceptance : September 5, 2011
  • Deadline of camera-ready version : October 7, 2011
  • Deadline for author registration : October 14, 2011
  • 1 day for workshops : December 7, 2011
  • 2 days for main conference : December 8-9, 2011

PUBLICATIONS :

Papers accepted for ICPADS 2011 conference and workshops will be published by the IEEE Computer Society Press. All accepted papers will be included in IEEE Xplore and indexed by EI. Best Paper Award and Best Student Paper Award will be awarded in the conference. Selected papers will be recommended by the program committee to a number of international journals.
