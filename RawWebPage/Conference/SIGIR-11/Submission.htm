Paper submission

All submissions have to be made electronically. The exact time of the deadlines corresponds to the last moment (23:59:59) of the indicated date in the latest time zone on the planet (i.e. Apia Samoa time).

Full papers (Deadlines Jan. 17, 2011 and Jan. 24, 2011)

To submit a full paper, one has to submit first an abstract by January 17, 2011. The full paper can then be submitted by January 24, 2011. The submission site for both the abstract and the full paper is http://sigir2011.confmaster.net/

Note: Please read the guideline for full paper submissions at http://www.sigir2011.org/content-guidelines.htm. The length limit is 10 pages.

Posters and demonstrations (Deadline Feb. 11, 2011):

Posters and proposals for demonstration should be submitted by February 11, 2011 respectively on http://sigir2011-poster.confmaster.net/ and http://sigir2011-demo.confmaster.net/.

Workshops (Deadline Jan. 28, 2011):

Proposals for workshops should be submitted by January 28, 2011 directly to the workshop co-chairs: Noriko Kando and Luo Si.

Tutorials (Deadline Feb. 11, 2011):

Proposals for tutorials should be submitted by February 11, 2011 to the Tutorial chair Yi Zhang.

Doctoral consortium proposals (Deadline Mar. 7, 2011):

Proposals should be submitted in PDF by March 7, 2011 on https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=sigirdc2011
