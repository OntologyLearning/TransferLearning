IPDPS 2011 CELBRATES 25 YEARS! YOU ARE INVITED

  • Monday evening dinner party, starting at 6:30 pm, with host H.J. Siegel celebrating 25 Years of IPPDS, including music, slide show, annual TCPP meeting, video clips, Top 10 Reasons for Coming to IPDPS, and more.
  • Wednesday banquet festivities with host Arnold Rosenberg toasting the community of academic and industry researchers who have sustained the 25-year growth of this event and the future computer scientists who will be part of "what's ahead" for our community.
