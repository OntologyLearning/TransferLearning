Quality Metrics


ACM Transactions on Information Systems (TOIS) publishes only the highest quality papers about the design and evaluation of computer software that helps people find, organize, analyze, and use information in a variety of media. No single metric indicates how well the journal meets this goal, so we provide a set of metrics.

Acceptance Rate

During the period July 2008 - July 2010, TOIS accepted about 10% of the papers submitted to the journal. Many of the papers submitted to TOIS do not fit the journal's editorial scope and thus are rejected after only a brief examination. The acceptance rate is about 24% for papers that fit the journal's editorial scope.
