Asiacrypt Conference

The Asiacrypt conference is an international conference on all aspects of cryptology. The Asiacrypt conference has been sponsored by IACR since 2000, and is held in a different venue each year. For information about organizing Asiacrypt in the future, contact the Asiacrypt Steering Committee representative in the Board of Directors.
