IACR Distinguished Lectures


IACR Distinguished Lectures have been awarded by IACR since 1994 to people who have made important contributions to cryptology research.

The venue of the annual distinguished lecture alternates from Eurocrypt to Crypto to Asiacrypt. The distinguished lecturer is chosen by Board of Directors the year before.
