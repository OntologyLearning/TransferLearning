Subscriptions

The price for submission will be made available as soon as the journal is in production.  You can subscribe the journal by:

  • the web
  • calling 1-800-342-6626 (outside the U.S., 1-212-626-0500), or
  • sending e-mail to acmhelp@acm.org (or acm_europe@acm.org).

Acquiring Individual Articles

All past articles published in ACM TKDD are available through the ACM Portal (TKDD gateway) and the ACM Article Delivery Service.
