    Broader Impact

  • Founded and leading Kumu Networks to transfer and commercialize my group SNSG's research on self-interference cancellation and full duplex to practice. The technology is broadly applicable across all radios; Kumu is developing products that use full duplex technology to significantly improve cellular spectral efficiency and broadband speeds.
  • Working with Google.org to transfer our research on OpenRadio and software defined cellular networking to practice via a broadband network deployment in Northern California. Our goal is to create open, inexpensive and re-usable network hardware and software substrates that underserved communities around the world can use to quickly deploy broadband wireless networks.
