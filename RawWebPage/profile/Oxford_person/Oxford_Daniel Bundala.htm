Daniel Bundala 
Doctoral Student
Department of Computer Science, University of Oxford
daniel.bundala@cs.ox.ac.uk

I am a doctoral student in the Department of Computer Science, University of Oxford working in the Verification Group. My supervisor is Joel Ouaknine. During my PhD I did software engineering internships at Google Research, NYC (summer 2013) and at Google Inc, Zurich (summer 2012). Before starting my PhD, I was an undergraduate at University of Oxford graduating in 2010 in Mathematics and Computer Science (1st class honours). I won silver medals at ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest and at International Olympiad in Informatics.

Research Interests
I am working on formal verification, especially on model checking, parameter synthesis, temporal logics, infinite-state verification, timed automata and counter machines. I am also interested in theoretical computer science (broadly conceived), combinatorial algorithms and sorting networks.
